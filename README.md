# Brazilian E-Commerce Analytics

## Overview

This project analyzes a Brazilian e-commerce dataset using SQL and Tableau to uncover sales trends, customer behavior patterns, delivery performance, and regional business insights.

The goal of the project was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence through data analysis and dashboard development.

## Business Objectives

* Analyze revenue growth over time
* Track order volume trends
* Evaluate Average Order Value (AOV)
* Identify top-performing regions
* Analyze delivery performance
* Explore customer satisfaction metrics
* Create an executive-level dashboard

## Key Insights

### Revenue Growth

Revenue increased significantly throughout the analyzed period, demonstrating strong business expansion and growing customer demand.

### Order Volume

Monthly order volume showed consistent growth, indicating successful customer acquisition and retention.

### Average Order Value

Average Order Value remained relatively stable, suggesting that revenue growth was primarily driven by increasing order counts.

### Regional Performance

Several regions generated significantly higher revenue than others, highlighting opportunities for targeted business strategies.

### Customer Satisfaction

Customer review scores were strongly influenced by delivery performance, emphasizing the importance of logistics efficiency.

## Business Recommendations

* Continue investing in high-performing regions.
* Improve delivery speed and reliability.
* Monitor customer satisfaction metrics alongside operational KPIs.
* Develop retention strategies for valuable customer segments.
* Explore opportunities to increase Average Order Value.
